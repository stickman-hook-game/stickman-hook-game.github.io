Counter-Notification Procedure

If content has been removed or disabled from stickman-hook-game.github.io due to a copyright complaint but you believe this action was mistaken or a result of misidentification, you may submit a counter-notice to request reinstatement of the material.

Your counter-notice must include:

Upon receipt of a valid counter-notice, we will promptly forward it to the original complainant. Unless the complainant files a lawsuit seeking a court order to prevent the content’s reinstatement within the legally prescribed period, we will restore the material in compliance with the DMCA.

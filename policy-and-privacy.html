Privacy Policy

Introduction

At escape-road-game.gitlab.io, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. We are committed to safeguarding the personal information you provide and ensuring transparency about how your data is collected, used, and protected when you visit or interact with our website. This Privacy Policy outlines in detail what information we collect, the purposes for which we use it, and the steps we take to maintain the security and confidentiality of your personal data.

Information We Collect

To deliver an optimized and personalized gaming experience, we may collect various types of information from and about you, including:

  • Personal Information: This includes any information you voluntarily provide, such as your full name, email address, contact details, and any other identifiers you submit when signing up for newsletters, registering on the site, submitting feedback, or contacting customer support.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect serves several vital functions, including but not limited to:

  • Operating, maintaining, and improving the website and its gaming services to ensure a secure, efficient, and enjoyable experience.

  • Sending you important notifications, updates, promotional offers, and responding effectively to your inquiries and requests.

  • Personalizing content, game recommendations, and advertisements tailored to your preferences and previous interactions.

  • Analyzing usage trends to detect fraudulent activity, enhance security, and optimize overall performance.

We employ cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information that enables us to optimize your browsing experience on escape-road-game.gitlab.io. Cookies help remember your preferences, authenticate your sessions, analyze traffic patterns, and deliver personalized content.
